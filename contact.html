Introduce Me

My name is Nabek Abebe,first year software engineering student at Addis Ababa Institute of Technology.I had the interest of taking software class since the age of 12 but it was late in my teen's that i actually got something to do about it. i started taking video tutorials and practice my coding skills.I have dedicated my life on improving my coding skill since.My work paid off and now I am a software engineering student in the the highest standard university in Ethiopia,Addis Ababa University. In my spare time I develop websites for individuals and companies such as school.

I moved on to study the software engineering in Addis, and began to grow my network.developing websites and branding work for friends and schools. Going to school during the day and cranking on design work at night. Not only was I learning the basics of quality programming during my classes, but I was also understanding what it meant to run a business in the outside world.

I love what I do. The combination of business goals, mixed with human necessity, mixed with solid design is what gets me going every day. Don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to chat about programming, life, or just have a good ol' fashioned conversation.
